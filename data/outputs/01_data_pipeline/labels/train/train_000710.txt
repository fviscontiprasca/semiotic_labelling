Venetia, a picturesque coastal town, features a captivating entrance to its scenic area, where the original village entrance has been transformed into a scenic gate and square. The entrance is paved with vibrant blue and white square tiles, effectively separating pedestrian traffic from the dedicated vehicle passage that connects to the waterfront highway at the rear. The surrounding vegetation has been carefully maintained to provide ample space for the design of the square and gate, creating a harmonious blend of nature and architecture.
